Our Story

We are so incredibly excited to share our story with you!

Jan 2022

First meet

Tyson and Lucie first met in January 2020. Tyson came back to Vietnam to visit his family and he met Lucie through bumble. Originally, they both planned to keep the relationship short and sweet. But after spending a month together, they decided to try long distance relationship

February 2020

Our story

When Tyson and Lucie said goodbye to each other in February 2020, they didn’t know it was the last time they’d see each other for a while. Their plan was to get meet up again in the Summer of 2020 either in Japan or Vietnam, but they were forced apart suddenly by the pandemic. There was enormous uncertainty about Covid19 specifically when it will be contained.

2021

Long distance

The couple tried to make things feel “as normal as possible” by talking daily, and daydreaming about future trips. But the pandemic was very difficult, Lucie admits. “Not knowing when Tyson could come back on Vietnam, constantly checking the news for immigration and travel updates, it was tough,” Lucie says. “We were both committed to making our relationship work but maintaining it long-distance was definitely taxing.”

2022

Proposal

Tyson planned a romantic staycation at Landmark 81 with Lucie. In the evening, he took her out for dinner and had the concierge at the hotel to decorate the room. When they returned to the room, Lucie was shocked and thought she went into the wrong room. There was candlelight pathway that leads to the window where there was balloons and cake. Tyson got down to one knee and asked her to marry him, and she said yes!
